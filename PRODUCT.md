# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Technical readers interested in AI, distributed systems, cloud-native architecture, and practical engineering insights. They typically read in focused sessions to quickly evaluate depth, credibility, and applicability.

## Product Purpose
This site exists to publish high-signal technical writing and establish trusted expertise through clear, compelling presentation. Success means readers can immediately understand Alex's domain depth, find relevant posts quickly, and leave with confidence in both the ideas and their execution.

## Brand Personality
Bold, experimental, and expressive, while still clear and trustworthy for expert audiences. The experience should feel confident and distinctive without sacrificing readability.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboard aesthetics.
- Corporate consulting templates.
- Any redesign that erases the existing cyberpunk identity and visual energy.

## Design Principles
- Distinctive expertise over generic polish: communicate strong point-of-view and technical authority.
- Clarity first, then expression: visual boldness must never undermine comprehension.
- Identity continuity: preserve and evolve the current cyberpunk aesthetic rather than replacing it.
- Signal density with navigable hierarchy: surface rich technical content in a scannable structure.
- Credibility through craft: consistent, intentional details should reinforce trust at every touchpoint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Meet WCAG 2.2 AA baseline across the site, including robust contrast and keyboard accessibility. Respect reduced-motion preferences and ensure interactions and content remain fully usable without motion.
